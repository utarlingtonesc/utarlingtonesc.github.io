Our Organization

Engineering Student Council is the umbrella organization to all engineering student organizations at UTA.

ESC is open for all engineering majors to stay informed about meetings and events for engineering student organizations as well as the College of Engineering.

ESC is the organizing group for Engineers Week events and hosts monthly meetings that are open to all engineering majors.
